About us

Haukai Restaurant specializes in Māori kai using local produce, based in the Bay of Islands.

Our menu aims to celebrate Māori food in New Zealand, by playing a leading role in keeping our indigenous food culture alive.

We use flavours foraged from the land and sea with a special focus on Māori and Polynesian ingredients.
